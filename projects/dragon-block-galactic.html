Features

Built like a full game

Not a texture pack with powers. Every system below is engineered, server-authoritative and already playable in development builds.

🧬 Create your warrior

A full character creator: 6 races including Saiyan, Namekian, Arcosian and Majin, per-race transformations, deep customization and shareable look codes.

⚡ Master ki combat

Blasts, beams, beam struggles, grabs and throws, barriers, auto-dodge and aerial pursuit. The server decides every hit, so fights stay fair.

🌌 Cross a living galaxy

Procedural planets with their own terrain, gravity, caves and secrets. Navigate with a holographic 3D star map and travel by space fold.

🐉 Summon the dragon

Hunt all seven Dragon Balls and speak your wish out loud: power, revival, immortality, knowledge and more.

⏳ Live a whole life

Your character ages in real time. Races have lifespans, old age is permanent and reincarnation starts the next story.

📈 Grow stronger

Six attributes feed real combat formulas. Learn skills from masters, take quests and train under high gravity.

💥 Leave a mark

Battles have consequences, up to the destruction of entire planets. The galaxy remembers what you did to it.

👽 Meet the natives

Every world generates its own alien civilizations, with unique bodies, traits and settlements to discover.

FAQ

Questions, answered

When does the mod release?

When it is ready. We do not announce dates, because we only ship systems that hold up in real play. Supporters follow progress from the inside and try builds first.

Is the mod paid?

No. The public version is free to download and play, always. Supporting funds development and unlocks perks like early access builds and closed betas.

How do I receive my supporter perks?

You enter your Minecraft nick and your Discord username during checkout. Your supporter role on Discord and your beta access are granted automatically, and dev logs and early builds are delivered through the community server.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed through a secure Stripe customer portal where you can cancel or change your tier in one click. You keep your perks until the end of the billing period.

What do I need to play?

Minecraft Java Edition 1.20.1 with Forge. Detailed installation instructions ship with every build.
